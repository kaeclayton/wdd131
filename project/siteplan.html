Sacred Stops: An LDS Temple Travel Journal

Site Name

Domain:sacredstops.com

Logo Idea:Mountain silhouette with a spire-shaped peak(jade) and wreath, paired with "Sacred Stops" in charcoal.

Site Purpose

A travel journal documenting visits to Latter-day Saint temples worldwide, featuring personal reflections, architectural details, and visitor tips. Targets members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and temple enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • "Which temples have the most beautiful grounds for photography?"
  • "What's the story behind the stained glass in the St. George Utah Temple?"
  • "Which temples have the most unique celestial room designs?"
  • "How can I prepare spiritually before visiting a temple for the first time?"

Color Schema

Deep Jade Green (#00603F): Headers, logo accents, links.

Charcoal (#333333): Body text, mountain silhouettes.

Cream (#FCFCEF): Page background (soft contrast).

Deep Plum (#6A0DAD): Accents for sacred elements, featured cards.

Typography

Playfair Display (Serif): Headers (elegant, reverent).

Lato (Sans-Serif): Body text (clean, accessible).
