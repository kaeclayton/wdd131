Mesa, Arizona, USA

Arizona is one of the "younger" states in the USA. It is known as the Grand Canyon State, being home to the famous wonder of the world, the Grand Canyon. While many people think of Arizona as purely a hot desert wasteland there are 3,928 mountain peaks with 26 of those being over 10,000 feet tall. Some other fun facts about Arizona is that we do not observe daylight savings and the state tree is the Palo Verde.
